About Us



Why Choose Us

Our platform is one of the first connecting farmers directly to the consumers. No middlemen mean that farmers get the best value out of their produce and consumers get the most fresh consumables at the best price


Our Goals

Our goal is to empower the farmers by eradicationg the middlemen who take away a lion's share of commission.


Our Values

Our values are based on traditional Indian culture where farmers are the "अन्नदाता" and must be protected. While, the consumers are the "देवता" and must be served.


Our Mission

We aim to generate an agrotech revolotion by incorporating innovative technological solutions in agriculture, enabling farmers to get their maximum share in the truest form.
